Admissions FAQ
- Eligibility: 10+2 with PCM for engineering streams.
- Required documents: mark sheets, transfer certificate, ID proof, passport photo.
- Typical counseling starts in June and closes in August.

Fee Structure
- Tuition fee per semester: INR 55,000.
- Hostel fee per semester: INR 28,000.
- Lab and exam fee per semester: INR 7,500.

Courses
- CSE: 4 years, includes AI/ML electives from 3rd year.
- ECE: 4 years, includes embedded systems labs.
- Mechanical: 4 years, includes CAD/CAM and manufacturing practice.

Cutoffs (General, previous year)
- CSE: 168+
- ECE: 154+
- Mechanical: 142+
