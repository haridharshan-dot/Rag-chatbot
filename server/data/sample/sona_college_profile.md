# Sona College of Technology (Autonomous)

Sona College of Technology (Autonomous) is a private college in Salem, Tamil Nadu, India.  
It was established in 1997 by Thiru. M.S. Chockalingam (Founder Chairman) and gained autonomous status in 2010.  
It is NBA accredited, ISO certified, and accredited with "A++" grade by NAAC.  
The college is affiliated with Anna University and approved by AICTE.

## Admission

The selection committee constituted by Sona College of Technology, as per institutional guidelines, selects candidates under this scheme.  
Selection is provisional and subject to confirmation by Anna University / Directorate of Technical Education (DoTE), Chennai.  
ME/M.Tech/MBA/MCA admissions are based on entrance marks (TANCET/CET/GATE/MAT) conducted by Anna University and Association of Management of Coimbatore Anna University Affiliated Colleges.

## Rankings

NIRF 2024 (Engineering): 151-200 band.

## Facilities

Sona College is in Salem ("Steel City"), Tamil Nadu.  
It is around 1 km from Salem Junction Railway Station and 3 km from Salem Central Bus Stand.  
The campus area is 34.5 acres with approximately 7,50,000 sq. ft built-up area.

### Library

The library includes books, journals, and non-book materials across science, engineering, technology, humanities, social sciences, and management.  
It has 80,000+ books, 2,500+ CDs, and 2,000+ national/international journals.  
It is an institutional member of DELNET, IIM(B), TERI, and INSDAG.

### Hostel

Separate hostel facilities are available for Indian and NRI boys and girls.  
Facilities include a large dining hall, swimming pool, gym, indoor and outdoor sports facilities.

## Scholarships

Sona publishes scholarship schemes under management, government, and private-sector channels.  
Examples include Merit Scholarship (tuition fee waiver), Merit-cum-Means Scholarship, Sports Scholarship, and category-based government scholarships for BC/MBC and SC/ST students.  
For latest eligibility updates (including first-graduate clarifications), students should verify through official scholarship/admissions support.
