Admissions FAQ
- Eligibility: 10+2 with PCM for BE/BTech admissions.
- Selection for BE/BTech first year is based on cutoff mark out of 200 (Maths 100 + Physics/Chemistry 100).
- Required documents: marksheets, transfer certificate, ID proof, passport-size photos.
- Typical counselling/admission windows are announced officially on admissions pages.

Fee Structure
- Tuition fee details are published in official admission and fee pages.
- Hostel fee details are published by the admissions/hostel office and may vary by room type.
- Fee waiver references are available under official Tuition Fee Waiver and Scholarship pages.

Scholarships
- Management Merit Scholarship (Tuition Fee Waiver): Rs 10,000 to Rs 40,000 per annum for eligible students.
- Merit-cum-Means Scholarship: Rs 5,000 to Rs 10,000 per annum (income and performance conditions apply).
- Sports Scholarship: Rs 10,000 to Rs 40,000 per annum based on sports performance.
- Government Scholarship (BC/MBC): SWS category, parent annual income below Rs 1,00,000 (apply through college via edistrict).
- Government Scholarship (SC/ST): SWS category, parent annual income below Rs 2,00,000 (apply through college via edistrict).
- Government Scholarship (SC/ST Fee Waiver): 100% fee waiver for eligible SC/ST students.
- Govt. Special Scholarship: SC/ST students in Sona hostel, parent annual income below Rs 1,00,000.
- First graduate scholarship: specific eligibility is not explicitly listed in the scholarship table; students should verify current first-graduate criteria with admissions/live agent.

General Info
- Hostel is available: separate hostel facilities for boys and girls are available.
- Campus hostel facilities include dining hall, gym, and sports support.
- Placement highlights published on official pages include 200+ recruiters, 37 LPA highest international CTC, 19.54 LPA highest domestic CTC, and 98k/month highest stipend.
- Batch-wise placement details are published in official placement year pages.

Special Cases
- For complex admission cases like gap years, sports quota, or other quota clarifications, students should connect to live admissions support.
